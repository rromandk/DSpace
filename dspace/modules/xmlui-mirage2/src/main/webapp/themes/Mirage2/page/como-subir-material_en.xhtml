¿How to upload material?

If you are a researcher or teacher at the Universidad Nacional de Entre Ríos or have been in the past, you can contribute your works to the RIUNER repository by performing a simple procedure called self-archiving. The same can be done from anywhere with internet access and allows you to send the material in digital. To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Login to the site, if you are not yet a user, you can register here.
  2. Access the "Submissions" section. Once there, the system will guide you through a series of simple steps.
  3. Complete a form with the data of the work to be added.
  4. Choose the relevant license of use.

Once RIUNER receives the material after a brief check, it will be published in the repository and you will be duly notified.
